How Does Prostadine Work?

To understand the mechanism, it helps to know what drives prostate enlargement in the first place. The primary culprit is dihydrotestosterone (DHT), a hormone derived from testosterone via the enzyme 5-alpha reductase. Elevated DHT causes prostate cells to multiply, leading to the benign enlargement that squeezes the urethra and disrupts urinary flow. Secondary factors include chronic low-grade inflammation and oxidative stress in the pelvic region.
